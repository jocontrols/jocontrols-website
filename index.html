We are a team of engineers specialising in Electrical and Controls & Instrumentation Design and Software Development. Our primary service is adding value to projects requiring our expertise in producing electrical CAD design for power distribution and PLC-based control systems in accordance with relevant standards; PLC, HMI and all controls and intrumentations specifications, configuration and programming, followed by testing and qualifying the final product.

Our engineers operate on an hourly or daily contractual basis or fixed price. Work can be undertaken at either our own office or at customer's premises. We are dedicated to maintaining high quality work standards and can offer a complete project life cycle.
